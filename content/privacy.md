**Last updated: July 20, 2026**

This page explains what personal data this blog (`blog.theopnv.com`) processes, why, and what rights you have under the GDPR.

## Data controller

Theo Penavaire, sole author and operator of this blog.
Contact: [support@theopnv.com](mailto:support@theopnv.com)

## Cookies

This site does not set any cookies on a normal page visit. Two features are opt-in and only load third-party scripts (which may set cookies) after you explicitly interact with them:

- **Comments**: post pages show a "Load comments" button. Clicking it loads a [giscus](https://giscus.app) widget backed by GitHub Discussions. Nothing loads from giscus.app or github.com until you click that button.
- **Newsletter form**: focusing the email field loads Brevo's form script and Cloudflare Turnstile (used to block spam signups). Nothing loads from those domains until you interact with the form.

## What's processed, and why

| Data | Purpose | Legal basis | Processor |
|---|---|---|---|
| IP address (server/hosting logs) | Serving the site, security | Legitimate interest | Vercel |
| Aggregated visit stats (page views, referrer, country) | Understanding readership | Legitimate interest | Umami (cookieless; no cross-site tracking) |
| Email address | Sending the newsletter | Consent (you submit the form) | Brevo (Sendinblue SAS) |
| Form interaction signals | Blocking automated spam submissions | Legitimate interest | Cloudflare (Turnstile) |
| GitHub identity, comment content | Publishing blog comments | Consent (you click "Load comments" and sign in) | giscus / GitHub |

Umami does not use cookies or local storage and does not track you across sites; it briefly uses your IP address to compute a daily, salted visitor count, then discards it.

## Data retention

- Newsletter emails are kept until you unsubscribe (every email includes an unsubscribe link) or request deletion.
- Analytics data is aggregated and not tied to an identifiable individual.
- Comments you post via giscus live in the linked GitHub repository's Discussions and are retained until you or GitHub remove them.

## International transfers

Some processors above (Cloudflare, GitHub, Brevo) may process data outside the EU/EEA. Where that happens, it relies on the processor's own GDPR safeguards (e.g. Standard Contractual Clauses).

## Your rights

Under the GDPR, you can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, object to or restrict its processing, and ask for a copy in a portable format. To exercise any of these, email [support@theopnv.com](mailto:support@theopnv.com).

## Changes to this policy

This page may be updated as the site's tooling changes. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
